Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

Your privacy is the core of Endi. This application is designed to help you monitor energy levels. This policy explains how your information is handled.

1. Data Collection and Storage

The app is built on a privacy-first, local-only architecture.

  • Local Storage: All data you input - including energy levels, activity logs, and timestamps - is stored exclusively in a SQLite database on your physical device.
  • Encryption: The database is encrypted using a randomly generated password. This password is known only to you and is securely stored locally on your device.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not maintain any external servers. Your health data is never uploaded, synced, or backed up to any cloud service by this app.

2. Information Sharing

Because we do not have access to your data, we cannot share it with third parties.

  • User Control: Only you can choose to share your data (e.g., showing the app to a doctor or manually exporting the database). We strongly recommend against sharing your encryption password with anyone.
  • No Third-Party Access: No advertisers, health organizations, or government agencies have access to your data through this app.

3. Internet Connectivity

The app is designed to function entirely offline.

  • No Data Transmission: The app does not require an internet connection to record or display your energy levels.
  • Analytical Data: The only data that may be sent to an external server is anonymous analytical data, such as crash logs. These logs are used solely to improve app stability and do not contain any personal health data, energy logs, or sensitive identifiers.

4. Security

While industry-standard encryption is used to protect your database, the security of your data also depends on the security of your device.

  • User Responsibility: To prevent unauthorized access, we recommend using device-level security (PIN, biometrics, or patterns).
  • Breach Risk: In the event of a device theft or a specialized hack, your data's safety relies on the strength of the local encryption and your device's own security layers.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You are in complete control of your data.

  • Deletion: You can delete your data at any time by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling the app.
  • Permanent Removal: Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered by us, as we do not keep backups or copies on any external server.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
